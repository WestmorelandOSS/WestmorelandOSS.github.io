Who we are

We focus on software craftsmanship, building open source software and strengthening a community of developers through collaboration. Collectively we volunteer our skills to the needy and provide education to the beginner and the experienced. We are a community of driven, passionate professionals and novices who know we can change the world with our creativity and hard work.

If this sounds like the place for you, bring your skills and passion, the work is on us.
